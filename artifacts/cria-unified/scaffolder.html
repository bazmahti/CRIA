Scaffolder boundary: This tool fills clerical fields only (experiment ID, timestamp, question echo). All substantive fields — dissonance budget, position-privilege balance, channels, patterns, observer note — are left blank for you to complete. The annotations explain what each field controls; they do not propose values.

This friction is intentional. Substantive fields are research-design decisions, not boilerplate. A separate frame inventory describes what the question's language presupposes — it is descriptive, not prescriptive.
